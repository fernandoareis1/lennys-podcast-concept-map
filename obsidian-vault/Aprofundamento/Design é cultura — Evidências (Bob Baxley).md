# 📜 Design é cultura — Evidências (Bob Baxley)

**↑ Card:** [[Design é cultura, não serviço]] · **Tema:** [[Design — Cultura & papel do design]] · **Camada:** L4 (Aprofundamento)
**Fonte:** [[Bob Baxley]] — *35 years of product design wisdom* (1:41:59)

> Citações longas sobre cultura, valores e o papel do design. Versão curta no [[Design é cultura, não serviço|card]].

---

## O que dura é a cultura, não o produto
Steve Jobs listava como orgulho não só Apple II, Mac, iPod, iPhone — mas a **própria Apple**:

> "Everything we make is going to be a doorstop in three years, but the stuff you guys make [Pixar] they're still going to be watching in 100 years." *(05:33)*

Os produtos são efêmeros; o que persiste é "a way of making decisions, a way of behaving, a way of seeing the value of technology in the world (…). The whole company is constantly asking: how can the thing I'm working on be a little bit better?" *(05:33–06:16)*

## O que é design
> "Design is trying to imagine the future you want to live in and then take the steps to make it real." *(00:17)*

E uma **obrigação moral**: "Almost everyone now is going to have hundreds of interactions with a phone or computer (…) a lot of those interactions are not going to be great. We have an obligation as product people to put that emotional energy back into people's lives." *(00:00)*

## Design-led ≠ designer-led
> "Saying a company is design-led does not mean it's designer-led. I've never seen somebody graft it on after the fact. It's there at the beginning in the root DNA or doesn't exist." *(00:17, 14:32)*

No Vale, design vira "service organization (…) catching things before it goes out the door — not actually part of the development process." Ao entrevistar, Baxley procura se os founders têm uma **história genuína** de por que acreditam em design (caso ThoughtSpot / Ajeet Singh + IDEO). "Why would you want to work in a place that doesn't value the thing that you do?" *(13:30–15:42)*

## Bouncing off culture (valores vs comportamentos)
Baxley "bateu na cultura" do Pinterest ao importar os comportamentos diretos da Apple. A lição:

> "Most likely the new place hires you because of the values of the organization you left, but not the behaviors. (…) hold onto the values, but think how are those values best expressed in this culture." *(08:23)* → [[Manter valores, não comportamentos]]

E a humildade: "Your career's not your life. (…) The job of a startup is to grow the founder so they can continue to lead the startup. (…) A lot of times people get outgrown by the role." *(11:32–13:11)*
